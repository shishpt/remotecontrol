About Us

Restaurant Italien is one of the oldest operating restaurants in Reykjavík.
It is located in the heart of the city, on Laugavegur 11. The restaurant was owned by two Italian chefs, Tino and Fabio, since 1991, but in April 2022 a new owner took over.

The restaurant's premises, with its famous pink facade and typical Italian fountain, has become a definite landmark in the center of Reykjavík. With the change of ownership, changes were made to the menu, it was updated and renewed, but most of the good old dishes are still on the menu. Welcome to us in Italy. We look forward to welcoming you!
